Privacy Policy

Adventure Club  ·  Effective Date: May 18, 2025

Overview

Adventure Club ("the App") is a private membership application used to manage vehicle reservations, club content, member communications, and points. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and who can access it.

The App is available exclusively to invited members. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information – your name, email address, and account role (member or admin).
  • Contact details – phone number and home or delivery address, if you choose to add them to your profile. These are optional and used solely to facilitate vehicle drop-off reservations.
  • Reservation data – details of vehicle and golf simulator reservations you create, including dates, the resource reserved, fulfillment preference, and any notes you provide.
  • Points activity – your points balance and transaction history associated with reservations.
  • Device token – a push notification token generated by your device so we can send you reservation reminders and status updates. This token is tied to your account and removed when you log out.
  • App feedback and requests – content you voluntarily submit through the vehicle suggestion, help request, and app feedback forms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your membership account.
  • To process and manage your vehicle and golf simulator reservations.
  • To send push notifications for reservation confirmations, reminders, and status updates.
  • To allow admins to coordinate drop-off deliveries using your contact details.
  • To track and display your points balance and reservation history.
  • To review member feedback and help requests submitted through the App.

Who Can See Your Information

You can see your own profile, reservation history, and points activity.

Admins and Super Admins can see all member profiles (including optional phone numbers and addresses), all reservations, all points activity, and all submitted feedback and help requests. This access is necessary to operate the club and fulfill reservations.

Other members can see that a vehicle is reserved on a given date but cannot see who reserved it or the reservation title. Their reservation details are visible only to themselves and admins.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database, Firebase Authentication, and Firebase Cloud Messaging), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Access to the database is enforced by security rules that restrict each user to only the data they are permitted to read or write.

We do not store passwords in our database. Authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication. Passwords are never accessible to admins or any member of the club.

Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, your device generates a unique token that is stored on your account in our database. We use this token to deliver reservation reminders, confirmations, and status updates directly to your device.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings. Your notification token is automatically removed from our database when you log out of the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase – authentication, database, cloud functions, and push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy →
  • Apple Maps – used to provide directions for drop-off reservations when you tap "Get Directions." No location data is collected or stored by the App.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your membership is active. If your account is removed by an admin, your profile and associated data are deleted from the database. Reservation records may be retained for club record-keeping purposes.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adult club members only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to your club administrator through the App's Help Request feature.
